Community Resources 4 Youth Program Details

Community Resources 4 Youth, Inc. was established as a repository of programs, services, and resources that serve the needs and interests of youth and young adults through four distinct yet related programs:

Education

Academic counseling and planning resources to increase and enhance learning and academic success.

  • Academic planning and counseling
  • Basic skills remediation/tutoring
  • GED preparation courses
  • College entrance testing (ACT/SAT)
  • College planning assistance (college application, developing personal statements, financial aid, and scholarship research)

Life Skills

Teaches and equips youth and young adults with basic and practical skills to help them make informed life decisionsprograms.html

  • Life Coaching
  • Financial Literacy and Understanding Credit
  • Budgeting and Money Management
  • Career Exploration and Job Readiness
  • Family Planning
  • Personal Grooming and Hygiene
  • Community Engagement and Networking

Mentoring

Promotes caring relationships with adult mentors from various backgrounds and professions for youth who may lack the presence of a positive role model.

  • Self-Esteem & Self-Empowerment
  • Peer Pressure & Bullying

Apply to become a volunteer mentor.

Wellness

Engages youth and young adults in activities that promote health and physical fitness, social and mental well-being, build self-esteem, and promote teambuilding.

Apply for our programs.
