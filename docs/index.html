Community Resources 4 Youth

Equipping and empowering youth and young adults to overcome adversity and challenges to positively impact and transform young lives and futures.

Community Resources 4 Youth, Inc. was established as a repository of programs, services, and resources that serve the needs and interests of youth and young adults through four distinct yet related programs.

Our programs

Education

Academic counseling and planning resources to increase and enhance learning and academic success.

Life skills

Teaches and equips youth and young adults with basic and practical skills to help them make informed life decisions.

Mentoring

Promotes caring relationships with adult mentors from various backgrounds and professions for youth who may lack the presence of a positive role model.

Wellness

Engages youth and young adults in activities that promote health and physical fitness, social and mental well-being, build self-esteem, and promote teambuilding.

Our objectives

Provide education and learning outcome assistance.

Support the development of positive, nurturing relationships with adults.

Encourage the development of conflict resolution, leadership, and collaboration skills.

Promote the physical, mental, and social well-being of youth and young adults

Our values

Commitment

We intend to contribute to the betterment of youth and young adults through programs that educate, equip, and empower them.

Support

We want to instill hope and build confidence in youth and young adults that motivates them to reach their full potential.

Investment

We want to give youth and young adults the tools and resources they need to learn and contribute to their own development and success.
